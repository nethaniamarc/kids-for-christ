BIBLE CURRICULUM

This 12-month Bible reading plan is designed for Kindergarten to 4th grade. Each month features a biblical theme, with 4 weekly story focuses and 5 short Bible passages per week to read. All passages are selected from child-friendly narrative sections in the Bible.

The Beginning of All Things (Genesis 1–11)

    Lesson 1: God Creates the World

    Reading: Genesis 1:1–31
    Theme: God is the Creator of everything.
    Memory Verse: Genesis 1:1 — “In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.”

    Lesson 2: Adam and Eve in the Garden

    Reading: Genesis 2:4–25; 3:1–13
    Theme: God wants us to obey Him.
    Memory Verse: Romans 5:19 — “For as by one man’s disobedience many were made sinners...”

    Lesson 3: Cain and Abel

    Reading: Genesis 4:1–16
    Theme: God looks at our hearts, not just our actions.
    Memory Verse: Proverbs 4:23 — “Above all else, guard your heart...”

    Lesson 4: Noah and the Ark

    Reading: Genesis 6:5–22; 7:1–24
    Theme: God saves those who trust and obey Him.
    Memory Verse: Hebrews 11:7 — “By faith Noah... built an ark to save his family.”

The Family of Promise (Genesis 12–50)

    Lesson 5: God’s Promise to Abraham

    Reading: Genesis 12:1–9
    Theme: God keeps His promises.
    Memory Verse: Genesis 12:2 — “I will make you into a great nation and I will bless you.”

    Lesson 6: Isaac Is Born

    Reading: Genesis 21:1–7
    Theme: God’s timing is always right.
    Memory Verse: Luke 1:37 — “For nothing will be impossible with God.”

    Lesson 7: Jacob and Esau

    Reading: Genesis 25:19–34; 27:1–40
    Theme: God’s plan is greater than our mistakes.
    Memory Verse: Romans 8:28 — “All things work together for good to those who love God.”

    Lesson 8: Joseph’s Dreams and Troubles

    Reading: Genesis 37:1–28
    Theme: God is with us even in hard times.
    Memory Verse: Genesis 50:20 — “You meant evil against me, but God meant it for good.”

From Slavery to Freedom (Exodus – Joshua)

    Lesson 9: Moses Is Called by God

    Reading: Exodus 3:1–14
    Theme: God calls us for His purpose.
    Memory Verse: Exodus 3:14 — “I AM WHO I AM.”

    Lesson 10: The Ten Plagues and the Passover

    Reading: Exodus 7–12 (selected verses)
    Theme: God shows His power and delivers His people.
    Memory Verse: Exodus 12:13 — “When I see the blood, I will pass over you.”

    Lesson 11: Crossing the Red Sea

    Reading: Exodus 14:13–31
    Theme: God makes a way when there seems to be no way.
    Memory Verse: Exodus 14:14 — “The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still.”

    Lesson 12: The Ten Commandments

    Reading: Exodus 20:1–17
    Theme: God gives us rules for living right.
    Memory Verse: Psalm 119:11 — “I have hidden your word in my heart.”

The Promised Land and the Judges (Joshua – Ruth)

    Lesson 13: Joshua and the Walls of Jericho

    Reading: Joshua 6:1–20
    Theme: Obedience brings victory.
    Memory Verse: Joshua 1:9 — “Be strong and courageous… for the Lord your God is with you.”

    Lesson 14: Deborah Leads Israel

    Reading: Judges 4:1–16
    Theme: God can use anyone who trusts Him.
    Memory Verse: Psalm 27:1 — “The Lord is my light and my salvation.”

    Lesson 15: Gideon’s 300 Men

    Reading: Judges 7:1–22
    Theme: God gives victory through faith, not strength.
    Memory Verse: Zechariah 4:6 — “Not by might, nor by power, but by my Spirit.”

    Lesson 16: Ruth and Boaz

    Reading: Ruth 1–4 (selected verses)
    Theme: God rewards loyalty and kindness.
    Memory Verse: Ruth 1:16 — “Your people will be my people, and your God my God.”

Kings and Prophets (1 Samuel – 2 Kings)

    Lesson 17: Samuel Listens to God

    Reading: 1 Samuel 3:1–21
    Theme: Listen when God speaks.
    Memory Verse: Psalm 119:105 — “Your word is a lamp to my feet.”

    Lesson 18: David and Goliath

    Reading: 1 Samuel 17:1–50
    Theme: Faith is stronger than fear.
    Memory Verse: 1 Samuel 17:47 — “The battle is the Lord’s.”

    Lesson 19: Solomon’s Wisdom

    Reading: 1 Kings 3:3–14
    Theme: True wisdom comes from God.
    Memory Verse: Proverbs 9:10 — “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom.”

    Lesson 20: Elijah and the Prophets of Baal

    Reading: 1 Kings 18:20–39
    Theme: The Lord alone is God.
    Memory Verse: Psalm 46:10 — “Be still and know that I am God.”

The Exile and Return (Daniel – Nehemiah)

    Lesson 21: Daniel in the Lions’ Den

    Reading: Daniel 6:1–23
    Theme: Stay faithful no matter what.
    Memory Verse: Daniel 6:23 — “He trusted in his God.”

    Lesson 22: Esther Saves Her People

    Reading: Esther 4:10–17
    Theme: God places us where we can make a difference.
    Memory Verse: Esther 4:14 — “For such a time as this.”

    Lesson 23: Nehemiah Rebuilds the Wall

    Reading: Nehemiah 2:11–20; 6:15–16
    Theme: God helps us complete His work.
    Memory Verse: Philippians 4:13 — “I can do all things through Christ.”

    Lesson 24: Prophecies of the Coming Savior

    Reading: Isaiah 9:2–7; Micah 5:2
    Theme: God promised to send a Savior.
    Memory Verse: Isaiah 9:6 — “For unto us a child is born...”

The Savior and His Mission (Matthew – Acts)

    Lesson 25: The Birth of Jesus

    Reading: Luke 2:1–20
    Theme: Jesus is God’s promised Son.
    Memory Verse: Luke 2:11 — “A Savior has been born to you; He is Christ the Lord.”

    Lesson 26: The Great Commission

    Reading: Matthew 28:16–20; Acts 1:8
    Theme: We are called to share the Good News.
    Memory Verse: Matthew 28:19 — “Go and make disciples of all nations."
