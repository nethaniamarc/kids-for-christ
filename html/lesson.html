Elementary School Lessons

This 12-month Bible reading plan is designed for Kindergarten to 4th grade. Each month features a biblical theme, with 4 weekly story focuses and 5 short Bible passages per week to read. All passages are selected from child-friendly narrative sections in the Bible.

The Beginning of All Things (Genesis 1–11)

    Lesson 1: God Creates the World

    Reading: Genesis 1:1–31
    Theme: God is the Creator of everything.
    Memory Verse: Genesis 1:1 — “In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.”

    Lesson 2: Adam and Eve in the Garden

    Reading: Genesis 2:4–25; 3:1–13
    Theme: God wants us to obey Him.
    Memory Verse: Romans 5:19 — “For as by one man’s disobedience many were made sinners...”

    Lesson 3: Cain and Abel

    Reading: Genesis 4:1–16
    Theme: God looks at our hearts, not just our actions.
    Memory Verse: Proverbs 4:23 — “Above all else, guard your heart...”

    Lesson 4: Noah and the Ark

    Reading: Genesis 6:5–22; 7:1–24
    Theme: God saves those who trust and obey Him.
    Memory Verse: Hebrews 11:7 — “By faith Noah... built an ark to save his family.”
