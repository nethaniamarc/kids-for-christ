Meet Mrs.Soledad

Hi! I’m Soledad — I’ve been part of the NJEBC family for over 30 years and have been happily married for 33. I’m a proud mom of five grown children, two adoptive children, and two beautiful grandchildren.

I truly love working with kids and helping them learn about God’s love while also growing into kind, responsible people who make a difference in their communities.
