Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Kids for Christ?

    Great question! It's an organization that started over 20 years at New Jerusalem Evangelical Church and seeks to teach children about the bible through lessons and games.

  • Is there an in-person meet-up?

    Absolutely! The lessons will be explored further in a weekly bible study at New Jerusalem Evangelical Baptist Church. No need to register, just show up!

  • Who are the lessons for?

    For children of all ages. They are divided in elementary, middle, and high school to fit the biblical knowledge that students may have at certain ages. But if the content is too easy or too hard, feel free to adjust by going up or down a school level.

  • What if I fall behind on the lessons?

    That's Ok! We understand that life can be hectic. Feel free to work at your own pace. Note however, that the in-person meetings follow a weekly schedule.

  • How many lessons should I do in a week?

    1 lesson per week. This lesson plan was designed to be completed in 1 year.

  • How can I help my student with the lessons?

    Before beginning a lesson, read the scripture prior to prime your child for what they will learn. And, since each lesson should be completed in a week, take the week to read through (and maybe even re-read) challenging materials.

  • Where can I find the schedule for in-person meetings?

    You may find the schedule located in the Schedule section. It should project all future meetings for the school year.
